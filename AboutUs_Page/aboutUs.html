Why Inova?

Since early 2000, INOVA has been arranging innovation camp all over Norway. The themes for the camps are totally different. All from solving food crisis to finding a replacement for plastic bags. At the camps the participants will be challenged with a problem, and the goal is to come up with the best solution.

INOVA is based around the concept of frugal innovation. That means the participants are engaged to find solution that are smarter and better than what todays solutions are. Even though today’s technology is advanced, there are billions of people that do not benefit from this because of the stiff price. We try to think of problems different to make the solutions more affordable and accessible. To do more with less.

If this sound interesting, we encouraged you to sign up for one of our camps. You will learn a whole lot, and we believe you will be the one to make the difference.
